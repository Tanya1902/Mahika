About Us

Letters for Change is a movement by the children, for the children. You may not be 18 yet. You may still be at school, but you are still aware of the issues that affect your life. And you want to talk about them. It may be about education, public transportation, environment, safety, and many other issues that affect all of our lives. Are you thinking about what one person or one letter can achieve? Mahatma Gandhi, Winston Churchill, Jawaharlal Nehru, Martin Luther King were all letter writers. And they brought change through the power of their words. The pen is indeed mightier than the sword.

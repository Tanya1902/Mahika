FAQs


Q: How can I donate to MMF?
A: Donations to MMF happen in a simple procedure wherein you can provide your contributions either directly through our website or through the campaigns we raise in fundraising platforms.

Q: Do I get a receipt of my donation?
A: Yes. Receipts will be provided for each donation.

Q: What is my donation used for?
A: All donations are used for coordinating and managing the letter exchange process that takes place.

Q: How do I know my donation is being used for the right cause?
A: We at MMF guarantees that each and every letter send by every kid reaches the proper destination for which the amount contributed will be us



Fundraise


Fundraising at MMF is all about you. Doing things your way help MMF make a difference. You can support MMF's initiatives by sponsoring our campaign or by holding your own fundraiser and starting the conversation to ensure children's voices are heard loud and clear.



Q: Why do we need your funds?
A: We require your funds so that we can ensure that the letters from the children reach their respective destination.

Q: How your support will change lives?
A: Fundraising is essential to organise and coordinate all letter exchanging processes that will fulfill the dreams of each and every child.
